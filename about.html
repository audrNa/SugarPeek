About Sugar Peek

Sugar Peek is an online shop which caters different market who wants to purchase customized cakes and pastries, established by Filipino owners.

It was established in August 2020, during the midst of the COVID-19 Pandemic. It was a very good timing and opportunity.

The owners teamed up with an online cake business since it was a family business. The company currently sells cakes and pastries, including Filipino and continental pastries. These cakes are also customized according to the customers' requests.

One of their business locations is in Dar es Salaam, Tanzania in East Africa.
